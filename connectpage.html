Hello there!
I'm Sidhya and I'm a Senior in the BFA Communication Design program with a Minor in Psychology at Parsons School of Design at The New School. I was also a part-time student at Cornell Tech studying product design & management with graduate-level students at Cornell Tech. I like to design products that visually look good and creatively solve complex problems. I love to brainstorm ideas on things I dislike and then create prototypes that might solve the problems. Some classes that I've taken are Product Development & Management, UX Design, Quantum Computing, Metaverse & AI, Psychology, Typography, Economics, and Design History. My professional goals are to become a successful Product Manager and help make products that make an impact.

During my free time, I like to play guitar and produce some music. When it comes to sports, I play soccer, badminton, and you can catch me running in Central Park and Hudson River Park. I'm also a novice photographer and I love walking around New York City with my DSLR and film cameras that "borrowed" from my dad. Thanks to college, I'm learning how to cook some decent and healthy food.

Some causes that I care about are making education and technology more inclusive and accessible to everyone, especially in developing countries like India. I hope to use design as a tool to have a significant impact on improving people's lives.
