Hello there!
I'm Sid and I'm currently a sophomore in the BFA Communication Design program at Parsons School of Design at The New School. I like to design products that visually look good and creatively solve complex problems. I love to brainstorm ideas on things I dislike and then create prototypes that might solve the problems. Some classes that I'm taking this semester are Interaction Design, Psychology, Typography, and Art History. I aspire to become a professional User Experience Researcher one day.

During my free time, I like to play guitar and produce some music. When it comes to sports, I play soccer and badminton. I'm also a novice photographer and I love walking around New York City with my camera. Thanks to college, I'm learning how to cook some decent and healthy food.

Some causes that I care about are making education and technology more inclusive and accessible to everyone, especially in developing countries like India. I hope to use design as a tool to have a significant impact on improving people's lives.
